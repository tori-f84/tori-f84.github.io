Bespoke jewellery,
inspired by the coast.

In my other life l've been working for a marine conservation organisation, and have been fortunate enough to travel to many far-flung places, all of which have the sea in common. The ocean has been a life-giver and constant source of inspiration, including for my designs.

I fell in love with chrysoprase, a particularly beautiful stone the hues, depths and peculiarities of which vary enormously between each piece, reminiscent of the colours of the ocean. My preferred setting is backless, which takes a little longer to create, but is worth it to show off both sides of the stones - often the back is a beautiful as the front!
